Biryani बिरयानी

Ingredients Steps

Description

Biryani is a spiced rice dish made with basmati rice, marinated meat, and aromatic spices. It is cooked using a layering method where rice and meat are combined and slowly steamed together. This gives the dish its strong aroma and rich, deep flavor.

The dish is commonly served with yogurt raita, salad, or a spicy curry. While there are many regional variations, chicken biryani remains one of the most popular rice dishes around the world.

Ingredients

  • 400 g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3–4 cardamom pods
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro and mint
  • Saffron milk (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash the basmati rice and soak it in water for about 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, garlic, ginger, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Cook the rice until it is about 70% done, then drain it.
  4. Heat oil or ghee in a large pot and fry the sliced onions until golden brown.
  5. Add the whole spices, then stir in the tomatoes and cook until softened.
  6. Add the marinated chicken and cook until the chicken is nearly done.
  7. Sprinkle in the coriander, cumin, and garam masala, mixing everything well.
  8. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken mixture.
  9. Add chopped cilantro, mint, and saffron milk if using.
  10. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat for 20–25 minutes until fully cooked.
  11. Gently fluff the biryani before serving.
  12. Serve hot with raita or salad.
